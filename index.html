Abstract

Let $SPP(n)$ be the set $\left\{\big(|A+A|,|A\cdot A|\big) : A\subseteq {\mathbb N}, |A|=n\right\}$, where $A+A$ is the sumset $\{a+b : a,b\in A\}$ and $AA$ is the product set $\{ab:a,b\in A\}$. We prove the value of $SPP(n)$ exactly for $n\le 6$, and compute a large subset of $SPP(n)$ for $n\le 32$. This is a dataset of \totalsize\ sets of positive integers that are addtiively and multiplicatively diverse. We do {\bf not} see evidence in favor of Erd\H{o}s's Sum-Product Conjecture in our dataset. We include a number of conjectures, open problems, and observations motivated by this dataset, and a large number of color visualizations.
